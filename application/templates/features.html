Warehouse Management

What is Warehouse Management?

Warehouse Management is an application that allows the suppliers to manage their stocks inside the warehouse. Suppliers can use Warehouse Managemenr to view and modify the amount the type of products they want to sell. The staffs and admins use our applications reach out to vendors and clients to connect them and manage them. A unique feature about Warehouse Management is Kafka Monitoring, where it is able to track the events of the users using the webiste in real-time
